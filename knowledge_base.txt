Internet Issue:
Restart the router, check ISP connectivity, and verify cable connections.

Printer Issue:
Clear the print queue, reconnect the printer, and reinstall printer drivers.

Login Issue:
Reset password, clear browser cache, and verify account credentials.

Laptop Heating:
Clean cooling vents, close background applications, and use a cooling pad.

WiFi Disconnecting:
Restart router, update WiFi drivers, and check signal strength.

No Sound Issue:
Check volume settings, restart audio services, and update audio drivers.

Screen Flickering:
Check display cables, update display drivers, and adjust refresh rate.

Slow Computer:
Remove unnecessary startup programs, free storage space, and run antivirus scan.

Software Crash:
Reinstall the application, update software version, and check compatibility issues.
